E.J. LeRoy

E.J. LeRoy is a Pushcart Prize nominated writer and poet with credits in several publications including Adventures Bookzine, Big Score Lit, The Carrier Bag, Fiction on the Web, Neon Dystopia, Neon & Smoke, NonBinary Review, Samjoko Magazine, Tales from the Crosstimbers, and Usawa Literary Review. LeRoy also has a science fiction mpreg novella published by The Whumpy Printing Press.
