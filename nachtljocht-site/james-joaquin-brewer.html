James Joaquin Brewer

Raised on the rural coast of U.S.A. Oregon (near the chanting and enchanting Sea Lion Caves), James Joaquin Brewer currently shelters in West Hartford, Connecticut. Among other places, his writing in a variety of genres has appeared in The Seattle Post-Intelligencer, The Write Launch, LitBreak, The Hartford Courant, Aethlon, Jeopardy, Rosebud, The Poetry Society of New York, Closed Eye Open, The Manifest-Station, Quibble, Open: Journal of Arts & Letters, BlazeVOX, Madswirl, Apricity, Lowestoft Chronicle, The Writing Disorder, and Twelve Winters.
