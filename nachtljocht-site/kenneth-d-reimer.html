Kenneth D. Reimer

Kenneth D. Reimer lives on the Canadian Great Plains with his wife, Lisa, and a cat named Nazca who likes to bite him on the leg. Whenever possible, he travels the world and attempts to record its wonders. His favourite art form is the short story, but he enjoys poetry. Occasionally, he writes longer fiction. Zero Time, his novel of time travel, is available on Amazon.
