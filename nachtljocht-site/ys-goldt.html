Ys Goldt

Ys Goldt was born and raised in the Everglades, where she still lives, sharing her home with things that may or may not exist. She has published a novel: Even if the Light Forgets: Volume I and two novellas, The Strange Mercy of Listening and An Index of Vanishing Part I. She is the founder and editor of nachtljocht.
