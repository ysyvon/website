Diana Parrilla

With a degree in economics and Japanese, Diana Parrilla writes fiction published by Inkd Publishing, Blue Planet Press, Murderous Ink Press, and others. She was awarded first prize in the 2024 Mollie Savage Sci-Fi & Fantasy Contest, received an Honorable Mention in the 2025 Writers of the Future Contest, and most recently earned a Silver Honorable Mention in the 2nd Quarter 2026 Writers of the Future Contest.
