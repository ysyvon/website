Ys Goldt is an artist and writer working across fiction, editorial, and worldbuilding.

Her work is guided by the same ethos as her writing: attention, devotion, psychological transformation, and the interior cost of being truly seen.

She is drawn to forms that hold tension between intimacy and distance, clarity and disquiet.

Ys is also the founding editor of nachtljocht, a literary journal for fiction.
