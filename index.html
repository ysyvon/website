Ys Goldt is a novelist and writer of speculative and literary fiction.

“We are asleep. Our life is a dream. But we wake up sometimes, just enough to know that we are dreaming.”

Her work centres on memory, residue, and the quiet violence of love—stories that move slowly, hold tension, and linger.

Her Latest Works Are: The Strange Mercy of Listening. Her debut novel.

and How Long Things Hold.
